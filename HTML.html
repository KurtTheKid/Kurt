Biography

Novak Djokovic, born on May 22, 1987, in Belgrade, Serbia, is a professional tennis player and one of the greatest of all time. He turned professional in 2003 and has since amassed numerous records and titles.

Achievements

  • 20 Grand Slam singles titles
  • Most weeks at No. 1 in ATP rankings
  • Winning all nine ATP Masters 1000 tournaments
  • Gold medal at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics
